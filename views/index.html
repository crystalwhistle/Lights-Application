YOUber’s algorithm takes advantage of Delphi’s San Diego demographics databases and compiles them together. For each area in San Diego, our algorithm looks at the population, income, Hispanic population, family size, vehicles, and number of workers in each location and compares them to the other locations. We determined what each type of Uber driver would find valuable and gave priority to regions that had more in those areas relative to other areas. Keep in mind that the data is not current and has changed since DELPHI received the data. We have listed what we believe each type of uber driver might value below.

UberX: population

UberXL: population, family size

UBER Espanol: Hispanic population, population

UBER Select: income, workers, population

UBER Black: Income, workers

UBER SUV: Family size, income
